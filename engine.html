Engine, drivetrain and suspension customizing

Unleash the Power of Your Vehicle's Potential!

Are you tired of your vehicle's stock engine, drivetrain, and suspension not keeping up with your adventurous spirit? Look no further, as Miyauchi Customs has got you covered! We specialize in providing top-notch custom engine, drivetrain, and suspension upgrades tailored specifically to your needs and desires.

  • Maximize Performance: Our experienced team of skilled technicians understands that every vehicle is unique, and we take pride in creating personalized solutions to unlock the true power potential of your ride. Whether you crave more horsepower, torque, or overall engine efficiency, our custom engine upgrades will help unleash the ultimate driving experience.
  • Enhance Durability: Take control of any off-road terrain or high-intensity driving situations with our custom drivetrain solutions. From strengthened differentials and axles to enhanced gears and transfer cases, we ensure maximum durability, efficiency, and reliability in every customized component. Drive with confidence, knowing that your vehicle is built to tackle even the toughest challenges.
  • Master Any Road: Don't let rough roads hold you back from exploring the great outdoors. Our custom suspension systems are designed to provide superior comfort, stability, and precision handling, allowing you to conquer any terrain with ease. No matter if you're a weekend warrior or an avid off-roader, our suspension upgrades will enhance your driving experience by leaps and bounds.

At Miyauchi Customs, we offer a wide range of related services to provide you with a comprehensive package that caters to all your vehicle customization needs. Our services include:

  • Performance Tuning: Fine-tune your engine's performance parameters, fuel mapping, and boost control to optimize horsepower, torque and fuel efficiency.
  • Exhaust Systems: Upgrade your vehicle's exhaust system for improved airflow increased power output, and a roaring sound that will turn heads wherever you go.
  • Brake Upgrades: Enhance your vehicle's stopping power with performance brake systems and rotors, boosting safety and control under extreme driving conditions.
  • Differential Upgrades: Reinforce your vehicle's differentials with stronger components to handle increased torque and maximize power distribution to the wheels.
  • Driveshaft Modifications: Custom-built driveshafts ensure reliable and smooth power transfer, especially in high-performance applications.
  • Chassis Reinforcements: Strengthen your vehicle's chassis with custom fabrication and reinforcements for added stability during high-speed maneuvers and off-road adventures.
  • Wheel and Tire Selection: Choose from an extensive range of high-performance wheels and tires that provide optimal grip, durability, and style to suit your driving preferences.
